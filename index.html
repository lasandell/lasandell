About Me

I'm a technologist who enjoys the high- and low-level details of systems. I'm currently the lead software engineer for a small payment processing company. I use .NET for my day job, but I am a Linux geek at heart. I'm the type of person who cares how their partitions are laid out on disk, if that means anything to you.

Aside from business applications, I am interested in electronics and ham radio in particular. Radio allows me to combine my interest in computers and electroncis with a fascinating physical phenomenon. Radio propogation involves technology, physics, as well as environmental phenoma such as space weather and the ionosphere. No longer just old guys talking behind microphones and big antenna towers, many of us trek out to parks and peaks to make contacts across the globe using small radios that output less power than a light bulb. Some of us still use morse code due to its interesting properties. Here is a map of my latest Parks on the Air activations!

Projects
